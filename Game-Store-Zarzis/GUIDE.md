## üöÄ Getting Started

### Login
Access the system using your staff credentials.
*   **Owners** have full access to settings, financial reports, and staff management.
*   **Staff** have access to daily operations (sessions, sales, clients) but restricted access to critical settings.

### Dashboard Overview
The main dashboard provides a real-time snapshot of the store:
*   **Active Sessions:** Number of consoles currently in use.
*   **Daily Revenue:** Total earnings (Cash + Points) for the current day.
*   **Quick Actions:** Shortcuts to start valid sessions or make a quick sale.

## üéÆ Session Management

This is the core feature of the application.

### Starting a Session
1.  Navigate to **Session Management** (Gamepad Icon).
2.  Click on any **Available** console card (Green).
3.  Select the **Session Type**:
    *   **Time-Based:** (e.g., 1 Hour, 30 Min). Auto-alerts when time is up.
    *   **Open Session:** Play as long as desired. Bill calculated at the end.
    *   **Per Game:** (e.g., FIFA Match). Fixed price per match.
4.  **Assign Client (Optional):** Search for a client to earn loyalty points.
5.  Click **Start Session**.

### Ending a Session
1.  Click on an **Active** console card (Red).
2.  **Step 1 - Summary:**
    *   Review the duration or number of games.
    *   *Note:* For "Per Game" sessions, you can adjust the actual games played vs. estimated time.
3.  **Step 2 - Payment:**
    *   Confirm the total amount.
    *   Click **Confirm & Pay** to close the session and update revenue.

## üë• Client Management & Loyalty

### Adding a New Client
1.  Go to **Client Management**.
2.  Click **+ Add Client**.
3.  Enter **Name** and **Phone Number** (Required).
4.  (Optional) Add email or notes.

### Loyalty Points
*   Clients earn points automatically for every Dinar spent.
*   **Redeeming Points:**
    *   In the Client's profile, click **Redeem**.
    *   Enter the points amount (e.g., to pay for a free game).
    *   The system deducts points and records the transaction.
